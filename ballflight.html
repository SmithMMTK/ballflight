D-Plane (verified): The ball launches at ~80% face + 20% path for a driver, or ~85% face + 15% path for an iron (steeper AoA → less path contribution). Spin axis tilt ≈ (face − path) × 4° (driver) or × 5° (iron), clamped to ±30°. Curve ≈ 3.5 yd (driver) or 4 yd (iron, more spin) per 100 yd carry per 1° of face-to-path. Initial direction = where the ball starts; offline = D·sin(launch) + curve. Face open to path → fade/slice (R). Face closed to path → draw/hook (L).
